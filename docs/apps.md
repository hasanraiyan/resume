# App Ideas

## 1. Renewly

A subscription and recurring-bills tracker.

### What it does

- Track recurring subscriptions like Netflix, Spotify, SaaS tools, domains, hosting, and memberships
- Show upcoming renewal dates
- Calculate monthly and yearly recurring spend
- Send or surface reminders before renewals
- Optionally sync payments into Pocketly

### Why it fits

- Pocketly manages general finances
- SnapLinks manages links and analytics
- Renewly adds another focused management app with strong tracking value

---

## 2. Taskly

A task and project management app.

### What it does

- Manage tasks, projects, deadlines, and priorities
- Organize work by status such as todo, in progress, and done
- Track productivity and completion trends
- Show upcoming deadlines and overdue work
- Support personal or small-team workflow management

### Why it fits

- It follows the same management + dashboard approach
- It adds productivity to the app ecosystem
- It can become a daily-use app, which is good for engagement

---

## 3. Assetly

A personal or business asset tracker.

### What it does

- Track devices, tools, software licenses, furniture, equipment, or digital assets
- Store purchase cost, purchase date, warranty, and current condition
- Track assigned owner or usage status
- Monitor depreciation or resale value
- Organize assets by category and location

### Why it fits

- It is another structured tracker app
- It has clear data, dashboards, and management flows
- It works for freelancers, creators, and small businesses

---

## 4. Habitly

A habit and routine tracking app.

### What it does

- Track daily, weekly, or custom habits
- Show streaks, consistency, and missed days
- Visualize progress with simple charts
- Group habits into categories like health, work, and learning
- Help users build routines and long-term discipline

### Why it fits

- It adds a lifestyle/productivity angle
- It is different from finance and links, so the app set feels more balanced
- It can have a strong mobile-friendly dashboard experience

---

## 5. Cliently

A lightweight client and invoice tracker.

### What it does

- Store client profiles and project records
- Track invoices, payments, due dates, and follow-ups
- Show revenue summaries and unpaid amounts
- Track project status and delivery progress
- Optionally connect incoming payments to Pocketly

### Why it fits

- It complements Pocketly well
- It is useful for freelance and agency-style work
- It turns the app collection into a more business-oriented suite
