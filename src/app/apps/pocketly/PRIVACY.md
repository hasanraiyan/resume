# Privacy Policy for Pocketly

**Last Updated: April 28, 2026**

Pocketly ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal and Financial Data

Pocketly is designed to help you track your personal finances. To provide these services, the App processes:

- **Transaction Information:** Amounts, dates, categories, and descriptions of your income and expenses.
- **Account Information:** Names of financial accounts and current balances.
- **Budgets:** Financial goals and limits you set within the App.

### 1.2 Connection Credentials

To sync with your personal financial data, the App requires:

- **API Base URL and Bearer Tokens:** These are used to communicate with your specified backend server.
- **Storage:** These credentials are stored securely on your device using platform-native secure storage (encrypted).

## 2. How Your Data is Used

- **Local Display:** To show you charts, summaries, and records of your financial health.
- **Backend Synchronization:** To fetch and save your financial data to the server URL you provide.
- **Financial Assistant:** If you use the AI chat feature, your queries are sent to your specified backend to provide financial insights.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Security:** We use industry-standard encryption for sensitive credentials stored on your device.
- **No Third-Party Access:** We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your financial data to outside parties. All data transmission occurs strictly between the App and the server URL you configure.
- **User Control:** You can clear your credentials and local data at any time through the App settings.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer or visit the project repository.
